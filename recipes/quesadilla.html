Quesadilla

Description

This is the best quesadilla recipe in the world.

It has very few ingredients, homemade sauce and is a very healthy meal that you can cook in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredient

  • wraps
  • cheese of your choice
  • chicken breast
  • corn
  • greek yoghurt
  • lemon
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken breast by cutting it with preferred dimensions. Marinade with salt, pepper, boya and some lemon
  2. Prepare the pan with some olive oil and cook the chicken between 5-8 minutes
  3. Throw in the pan the corn.
  4. After the chicken is done let it rest next to the pan and change it with the wrap.
  5. Put the chicken on the wrap, the cheese, some extra cheese and the greek yoghurt.
  6. Voilla the recipe was made in no time, now go eat some healthy quesadilla.
