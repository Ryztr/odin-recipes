Steak

Description

You wish to cook the perfect steak for you or your dear ones?

Now you can do it with this easy-to-follow recipe.

Ingredient

  • 350-400g steak
  • garlic
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Add the steak to the pan.
  2. Cook the steak for 1 minutes each side.
  3. Add the garlic on top of it and cook again for 5 minutes each side.
  4. After that let it rest for another 5 minutes on your cutting board.
