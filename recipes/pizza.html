Pizza

Description

Easy homemade pizza, healthy and few ingredients needed.

You only need 4 ingredients and 10 minutes of your time for this delicious pizza.

Ingredient

  • pizza wrap
  • cheese of your choice
  • tomato pasta
  • salami

Steps

  1. Add tomato pasta on the pizza wrap.
  2. Add the salami and cheese on top of it.
  3. Put it in the oven for 15 minutes at 180 degrees.
